Wellness Rituals
Your at-home anchors
🧖 Sauna Protocol
15–20 min, 2–3×/week. Best after a workout or in the evening. Sacred silence — no input required. Drink water before and after.
🙆 Evening Stretch (10 min)
Hip flexors, chest opener, spinal twist, hamstrings. Do it while watching TV. Consistency over intensity.
🌅 Morning Anchor (5 min)
Before your phone: one deep breath, one glass of water, one intention. Sets a regulated nervous system for the whole day.
✦ The Science of Starting
"You don't have to feel ready to begin. Beginning creates readiness."
Research shows the hardest part is always the start. Your brain predicts discomfort — but that discomfort rarely materializes. The goal isn't motivation. The goal is to move your body into position.
Procrastination Toolkit
Evidence-based tactics for right now
The 5-Second Rule
Count 5-4-3-2-1 the moment you think of a task, then physically move. Your brain can't argue with your body once it's moving.
Eat the Frog
Your most avoided task goes first — before email, before scrolling. Do it while your executive function is freshest.
Implementation Intention
'I will [task] at [time] in [place].' This specific formula increases task completion by over 200% per Gollwitzer's research.
Body Doubling
Work alongside someone — in person or virtually. Even a stranger's presence reduces avoidance significantly.
